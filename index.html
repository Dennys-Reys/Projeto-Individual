Unique Recommendations and Thorough Reviews

Of all the things that I love about music, I value it most for its versatility. Whether you are doing laundry or going for a run, focusing on work or partying with friends, there is a record that can provide the exact energy you need. My goal with Sad Pop Music is to expand my readers’ taste and highlight the beauty of music in all of its manifestations. That’s why my album reviews, concert updates and rotating list of new releases feature artists of all genres. Take a look around my site, and enjoy the new tunes.
